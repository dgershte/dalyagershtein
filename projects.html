Squiggle.
Winner of HackWaterloo

Winner of Best Hack at HackWaterloo 2014!. After doing a full 180 degree pivot from out original idea - an augmented reality game, Danny and I created this fun little hack in the last six hours before submission and ended up winning. The concept of the game is simple - choose a character and walk around a 3D world while drawing 2D images. This multiplayer game allows you to see what other people are adding to your 3D world in real time and makes for a very interesting experiment.
